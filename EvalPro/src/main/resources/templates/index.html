About University Examinations

The university conducts examinations twice a year for all courses. Students are required to follow the guidelines provided by the examination department to ensure a smooth examination process.

Revaluation Process

If a student is not satisfied with their examination results, they can apply for revaluation. The revaluation process involves a thorough rechecking of the answer sheets by the examination department. Please ensure you apply within the specified deadline.

How to Apply for Revaluation

To apply for revaluation, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official university revaluation portal.
  2. Log in using your student credentials.
  3. Select the subject(s) you want to apply for revaluation.
  4. Pay the revaluation fee online.
  5. Submit the application and keep a copy for future reference.
